Injuries in the National Hockey League

Ice hockey is one of the more dangerous sports out there. Players have to wear full body protection and the rate of injuries is still high when compared to other sports.

This visualization will examine the rate and severity of different injuries and will attempt to discover how the increased protection from modern protective equipment has affected these factors.
