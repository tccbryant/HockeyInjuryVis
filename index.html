Injuries in the National Hockey League

Ice hockey is one of the more dangerous sports out there. Players have to wear more protective gear than any other sport and the rate of injuries is still high in comparison. There are three positions in ice hockey that will be explored: Forwards, Defence, and Goalie.

This visualization will explore how players in each position get injured and the number and relative severity of those injuries.
